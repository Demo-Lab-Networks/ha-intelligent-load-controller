# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project harassment-free and welcoming for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity or expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected behaviour

- Be respectful, constructive, and open to evidence.
- Assume good faith; discuss ideas and safety implications rather than people.
- Protect household privacy and do not share credentials, addresses, or sensitive diagnostics.
- Prioritise safe operation over convenience or schedule pressure.

## Unacceptable behaviour

Harassment, discrimination, intimidation, personal attacks, deliberate safety bypasses, publishing private information, or disruptive bad-faith conduct are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Report concerns privately through the security/contact route once repository contact details are published. Maintainers may request correction, remove content, temporarily restrict participation, or ban contributors. Reports will be handled as confidentially as practical. This policy is adapted from the Contributor Covenant principles.
